Privacy Policy for QR-app

Effective date: October 2025

QR-app (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information when you use the camera to scan QR codes or barcodes.

1. Information We Collect

Camera Access:
The camera is used only for scanning QR codes and barcodes. We do not capture, record, or transmit any images or video frames outside your device.

No Personal Data:
The app does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Information

All scanning happens locally on your device. Scanned data (like URLs, contact info, or text) remains on your device unless you manually choose to share it.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to external servers or third parties.

4. Permissions

  • Camera: Used exclusively to read QR/barcode data.
  • Storage (optional): Only used if you import/export JSON backups; files remain private to your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app does not target children under 13 and does not collect personal data.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or privacy concerns, contact us at: support@tivlop.com
